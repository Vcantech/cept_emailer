About CEPT University

At CEPT University, aspiring thinkers and makers engage in rigorous, hands-on learning, interdisciplinary exploration, and community-centred innovation to shape sustainable, equitable built environments for people and places.

• CEPT University focuses on understanding, Architecture, Designing, Planning, Constructing, and Managing built environment.
• 60+ years of academic excellence.
• CEPT engages in diverse research and advisory projects as well as capacity-building programs through nine research centers in the University.
• The Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR) of the Government of India recognizes the University as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO).
• CEPT campus is located in the heart of Ahmedabad city.
